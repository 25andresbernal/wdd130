FAQ

What is the average cost to replace a driveway with interlock? To replace the whole driveway the cost for an average home witll range from $6,000 to $20,000. Can I finance the cost? We do not offer financing. It is cheaper to do small parts first or one big project? We offer discounts for jobs over $10,000. What stone work do you offer? We work with interlock, pavers, stairs, fountains, fireplaces, and stone tiles. How much does it cost to do a front stair replacement? The cost depends on the stone selected and the size of the job. The range you can expect is $2,000 to $12,000. How do I know I am getting the best price? We offer the highest quality of work with premium stones selceted by you. We gurantee our work will satisfy our clients or we will redo the driveway for you. No other company can say this. What options do I have when selecting stone? We offer hundreds of different stone options for you to choose from. We combine our designing expertise with these options to narrow the selection process for you. Do you offer maintance on the set interlock? Yes, we will re-sand your insterlock once a year for $200 dollars. How often do I need to replace my interlock? The interlocking stone will not need replacing for 20 years. Even after this time if maintaned properly the interlock can last a lifetime. How many workers will be on site? Our crews range from 3-4 all the way to 10-15 depending on the size of the job. Do I need to rent equipment? We will bring our own equipemnt. Do you offer any incentives for reffering a friend? Yes, we offer a 10% discount on any job that has a referral attached to it. The referral must be a current or future customer. If the referral becomes a customer after the job is completed then the disocunt gets returnedd to the orignal client.
